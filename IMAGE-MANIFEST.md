## Notes for Photographers

- All hero images should be high-resolution (min 2400px wide), landscape orientation
- Preferred subjects: autumn foliage paths, lake reflections, historic monuments, chapel exterior, tree canopy, spring blossom
- Color palette to target: deep greens, warm ambers, soft creams, golden light
- Avoid: people visible (except approved programming photos), commercial branding, cars
- All final images should be delivered as WebP or JPEG at 90+ quality, minimum 2000px on longest side
